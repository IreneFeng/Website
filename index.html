Preconditioning and Linearly Implicit Time Integration for the Serre-Green-Naghdi Equations
AuthorsLinwan Feng, David Shirokoff, Wooyoung Choi
DOI10.1016/j.jcp.2026.114849
Published20 March 2026
FieldNumerical Analysis · math.NA

Introduces a constant-coefficient preconditioner for the Serre-Green-Naghdi (SGN) constraint operator with rigorous bounds on the preconditioned condition number — incorporating bathymetry effects in two dimensions, guaranteeing mesh-independent performance for Krylov methods, and developing two linearly implicit time integration strategies for dispersive shallow water wave equations.
